About Me

My first interest in learning about the cloud started about two years ago as I was struggling to find a new challenge. I have come to learn/discover the many services that cloud providers make accessible to software developers particularly in the compute,storage and networking domains. With benefits such as scalability, availability and security in mind, I am exploring and testing my own application of Amazon cloud Services such as EC2, S3, Cloudformation, IAM, and many more. I look forward to finding a workplace where I can apply my newly acquired AWS cloud skills while keeping the door for improvements open.
